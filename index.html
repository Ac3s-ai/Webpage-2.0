Honorable Mentions

You Are An Idiot

Malware

YouAreAnIdiot was a trojan horse in 2009-2012. When the user goes on the website, the trojan would show text saying "you are an idiot" with 3 smiley faces, and the browser window would bounce around the screen. If the user tries to close the browser, it makes 6 more smaller windows which also bounce around the screen. Hitting Alt+F4 shows text saying "You are an idiot!". This trojan uses up all of the system resources, causing the computer to freeze..

ILOVEYOU

Computer worm

ILOVEYOU, sometimes referred to as the Love Bug or Loveletter, was a computer worm that infected over ten million Windows personal computers on and after 5 May 2000. It started spreading as an email message with the subject line "ILOVEYOU" and the attachment "LOVE-LETTER-FOR-YOU.TXT.vbs".

MyDoom

Computer worm

2004 saw the emergence of MyDoom, a notorious and sinister malware strain that spread rapidly, targeting technology companies like Google and Yahoo with Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks and turning infected computers into tools for spreading spam. Incredibly, MyDoom is still active today, and it has inflicted an estimated $38 billion in damage, marking it as one of the most destructive computer viruses ever.
